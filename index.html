About/Resume



Hi! I'm Dakota. I am a geographer with a love of GIS and everything spatial. Currently I am an Engineering Technician/GIS Specialist with the City of Fitchburg, WI. I recently graduated from the UW-Madison Advanced GIS Certificate course which puts a focus on web development and programming. I earned that certificate in May 2021. Bike Race Shaded Relief Map



This map is of a fictional multi-week bike race, that covers ~2,000 miles over the course of 21 stages (days). The route was created from a KML file drawn in Google Maps. The shaded relief data was manipulated in ArcGIS Pro, and then created in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. An interesting fact about this map is that the route passes through nearly every Wisconsin State Park!

Long-Form Infographic



For this project I chose to create a long-form infographic encouraging the use of electric vehicles. The bi-variate map was created in ArcGIS Pro, the graphs in Microsoft Excel, and the infographic itself in Adobe Illustrator. This project was very fun because it allowed a lot of creative freedom and design.
